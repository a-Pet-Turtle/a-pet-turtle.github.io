// about this site

The stylesheet and 3D banner for this site was created by Claude. The 3D Tutle emoji was created by me in Clay, the 3D voxel modeler. The structure and content of this site was mostly me.

This site also has some cool features:

  1. A grid background with dots that respond to mouse movement and clicks.
  2. A 3D rainbow header that responds to mouse movment with an animated cursor.
  3. A scanline effext that gives the site a subtle hacker theme.

The background dots can be pushed around by the mouse, but you can do a super push by just clicking. You can also pull the dots by draging.
